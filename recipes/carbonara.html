Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian dish out of Rome. The egg and cheese sauce topped with yummy guanciale make it a favorite across the globe!

Don't be scared of how the egg is cooked. Whisking over heat will cook it properly.

Ingredients (serves two)

  • 200g semolina flour
  • 95g water
  • 2 large eggs - yolks only
  • 45g finely grated pecorino cheese
  • 45g finely grated parmesean cheese
  • pinch of pepper

Steps

  1. make pasta dough
    1. Add flour and water to mixing bowl
    2. Mix until water is incorporated with flour
    3. Remove from bowl and knead until dough ball is properly formed
      4. Video on kneading dough
  2. Wrap dough in plastic and let rest for 30 minutes
  3. Turn your dough into the pasta of your choice!
  4. Dice the guanciale and cook on high heat. Set aside when done
  5. Boil water
  6. Mix eggs ,pepper, and cheese together in metal bowl. Once mixed, vigorously whisk while holding over the water
  7. Add two spoonfulls of guanciale fat to the sauce, keep whisking
  8. Put pasta in water, cook for 2-3 minutes
  9. Remove pasta, mix with sauce and guanciale
  10. Eat It!, Love It!
